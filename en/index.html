What is SCIS?

SCIS stands for Suwon Christian International School, a Christian alternative school established by Suwon Full Gospel Church. SCIS offers education from 1st grade to 12th grade based on the School of Tomorrow curriculum, grounded in a biblical worldview. Inspired by Isaiah 40:8, "The grass withereth, the flower fadeth: but the word of our God shall stand for ever," the school was founded on February 1, 2018, with the mission of nurturing global leaders who are truly needed in today's world, rooted in the eternal word of God.

Word From Our Founder

Home > Word From Our Founder
| Founder

Rev. Johan Lee

Today the field of public education is dominated by secular and unbiblical values. Biblical values are never to be compromised by secular values. It is because this world continues to reject the Christian faith for the reason of human reason. However, now I can visualize what I have been praying about which is through SCIS and God’s timing the next generation can receive a Godly education. I firmly believe that SCIS will cultivate plenty of faithful Christian leaders who will lead the 21st century through self-directed learning and character development education based on the biblical worldview. We aim to raise influential godly men and women with superb English skills in the world by using the utmost methods and making Godly choices based on the S.O.T. program. In addition, our great faculty and staff will do their best with God's heart as well. Please prayerfully consider sending your child(ren) to our school. May dreams, love, and blessings for your home.
